Honkai: Star Rail

Features the main character, who is referred to as the Trailblazer, travelling across planets through the Astral Express to help and connect the worlds while resolving disasters caused by "Stellarons" and other third-parties.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:"

  • "The environment design is very detailed."
  • "I can play it at my own pace as it is a turn-based game."
  • "The storyline of the game is mesmerizing."
  • "There are many sub-game modes in this game."
  • "There are many fun events that change every version."
